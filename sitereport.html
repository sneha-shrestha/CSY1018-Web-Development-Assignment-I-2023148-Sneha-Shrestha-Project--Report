Color Choices

The color blue is often associated with an open mind, imagination, freedom, and curiosity. This color tends to induce calmness and peace. As the sea and sky, as open as they seem,hides countless mystrereis within them, this color is perfect for communicating these traits. Blue and shades of blue are pleasing to the eye and invites the viewer to think deeper.Blue and shades of blueare pleasing to the eye inviting the viewer to pounder deeper. The reason for choosing blue is to induces clamness and the relaxation in the viewer's mind so user cas focus can on the key details of the website. Another fact about color blue is that it is the rarest in nature. There is no blue pigment naturally and every plant and animal that appear blue use trick of light to appear blue. Hence, the color also represents uniqueness and rarity and helps reflect those traits in the website.
